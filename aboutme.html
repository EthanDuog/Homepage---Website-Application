Israel - Robotics exchange program

In high school, I joined a Robotics club. I was selected to participate in an exchange opportunity to visit Israel to learn about building functional Robots.

At first, I was amazed by how Israel, a country half covered by desert, can produce such fresh and quality fruits. They even Israel desalinate ocean water to make it drinkable (tasted quite weird at first).

During this 3 week's program, besides learning about Robot programming and designing, I have made some great friends, who showed me about Israeli culture. They took me to so many places in Israel and touch me about their history and culture.

They made my try all Israel food (quite hard to taste) and brought me to the dead sea, where i took a bottle of salt water and keep it as a souvenir :)))

The Great Wall of China

I was always interested in Chinese culture and customs. In grade 10, I traveled to Shanghai, Beijing, and Xi'an with some of my siblings and aunts to explore Chinese culture.

We went to multiple aulics and its king grave-mound. It was seriously mind-blowing and I feel a bit of obsession. The history of humanity was brutal (caused by some individuals), filled with bitters and struggles that caused millions of deaths.

The Great Wall of China is built to protect territories from invasions. It was the bloody thing that, during the construction, it caused millions of death because of disease and exhaustion.

However, the beauty of ancient architecture is still presented and observed forever in time.

Thailand - Student Exchange Program

In high school, another exchange opportunity was given to me to involve in a cultural exchange program in Thailand. We were given a few weeks before the trip to prepare a performance and traditional outfits for the upcoming cultural-exchange event.

We stayed in a small province of Thailand for a week to meet and greet with other performer groups from different cultures. This was my first time meeting super friendly multicultural friends. We get along quickly and start to exchange knowledge about our culture. Such wonderful and kind-hearted people! They remain my friend until today

This experience transformed my personality toward being more integrated with friends from other cultures and overcoming language barriers. Which built me strong confidence when coming to Canada.
